Albatronics

Albatronics provides hardware and software applications and supplies R&D for leading telecommunications, military and high-tech companies in Israel including: Israel Aircraft Industries (ELTA, MLM, Lahav, MMG), Mobileye, Runcom, Verint. Albatronics has vast experience in defining, designing and developing Real Time (RT) applications up to system prototype and small scale production. Albatronics' competitive advantage resides in its capacity as an experienced and dedicated research facility.
